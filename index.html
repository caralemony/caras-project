Where Can I Code Today?

It's , check out what's going on today.


Here is London you can find great places to learn, collaborate and code almost every day of the week! Pick a day from the top menu to see what's happening.


Monday

Kickstarting the week we have Coding for Everyone run by Founders and Coders, a coding collective in Bethnal Green. With a strong collaborative and community based approach, you will always find the help and support you need. So find a buddy and get coding!

Typically split into 2 tables where they cover FreeCodeCamp and Codewars

Time: 6:30PM to 8PM
Location: 14 Palmers Road, London, E2 0SY

https://foundersandcoders.com/
https://www.meetup.com/founderscoders/events/
Tuesday

It's Founders and Coders again! Coding for Women runs on alternate Tuesdays, offering the same style as Mondays in an inclusive and supportive space for all women.

Same time, Same place.

Time: 6:30PM to 8PM
Location: 14 Palmers Road, London, E2 0SY
https://foundersandcoders.com/
https://www.meetup.com/founderscoders/events/
Wednesday

For some midweek mingling try Codebar.

Codebar is an initiative which pairs up newbie coders with experienced developers. Their coaches can guide you through anything you like, from solving challenges in Codewars and FreeCodeCamp to help with your own personal projects.

Codebar is fantastic for when there are concepts or issues that you cannot get your head around, so instead of driving yourself mad, get some professional help!

Time: 6.30PM to 9PM
Location: Various

Previous locations include: Google, Parliamentary Digital Service, Pivotal Labs.

Bonus: FREE FOOD! The hosts always put on a fabulous spread so ensure that you get there early for nibbles and networking.

They also run events across the UK and Europe so you may be able to join in wherever you are.

Find an event and sign up here; https://codebar.io/
Thursdays

As we move towards the end of the week it may be time to slow down, take a break, and perhaps listen to a nice talk.

General Assembly

As well as courses, General Assembly offer short evening talks which are FREE! From a wide range of topics and speakers, learn about UX Design, Data Science and everything tech.

Time: 6.30PM onwards
Location: The Relay Building, 114 Whitechapel High St, London E1 7PT

Browse upcoming events here https://generalassemb.ly/education?format=events

The Royal Institution

And a mighty fine institution it is too. A cornerstone of British scientific discovery since 1799 it plays host to scientific talks every weekday. With talks on subjects such as Quantum Computing to Sleep, it has something to get you thinking.

Time: 7PM onwards
Location: 21 Albemarle Street, London W1S 4BS
Have a look here http://www.rigb.org/whats-on
Friday

Happy Friday! Woop it’s time for the weekend! If you don’t fancy a night on the town, how about trying some online coding resources and you can code whenever and wherever you are.

FreeCodeCamp

Our MOOC (massive open online course) of choice! FreeCodeCamp is a comprehensive web development learning resource. Starting with HTML, CSS, JQuery and moving on to Javascript and more complex features, this course offers a massive online community with lots of help and resources.

Have a go here; https://www.freecodecamp.org

Codeacademy

Another fantastic MOOC is Codeacademy. Fancy learning the ropes of Git, or trying your hand at Ruby and Ruby on Rails? Offering a wide range of courses on different topics and coding languages, there is something for everyone.

https://www.codecademy.com/

Codewars

Put your coding prowess to the test in the Codewars dojo. Complete and create Katas (challenges) to hone your skills.

Fight it out at https://www.codewars.com/

YouTube

Not just for cat videos! Youtube is a great learning resource with Youtubers offering step by step guides to help you in your coding journey.

Learn some of the fundamentals of programming from real lectures at MIT. Check it out here; https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=k6U-i4gXkLM
Weekends

If the call of the lie in is not too strong, check out what’s happening this weekend!

Codeacademy London

Join a Codeacademy Hackathon. Drop in from 10AM onwards for a day of coding challenges. The focus varies week by week but include FreeCodeCamp and Codewars

Time: 10AM Onwards on Saturdays
Location: Citizen M, 6 Holywell Lane EC2A 3ET

NodeGirls

Nodegirls offers Javascript and Node.js workshops for women. From beginner to more experienced developers, there is a workshop for you.

Time: various Saturdays throughout the year.
Location: Various across the UK and the world!
